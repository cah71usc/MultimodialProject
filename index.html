Preventing Wildfires: Our Role and Responsibility

Did You Know?

Humans are responsible for nearly 85% of all wildfires in the United States. From unattended campfires to discarded cigarettes, our actions have significant consequences.

Steps You Can Take

  • Completely extinguish campfires before leaving them.
  • Never discard cigarettes or matches on the ground.
  • Follow local guidelines for outdoor burning.
  • Maintain equipment and vehicles to prevent sparks.
  • Report unattended fires immediately.
