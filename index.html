Preventing Wildfires Together

Over 85% of wildfires in the U.S. are caused by human activities. Let's change that.

Causes of Wildfires

Human actions like unattended campfires, discarded cigarettes, and fireworks can ignite devastating wildfires. Understanding these causes is the first step to prevention.

What You Can Do

  • Completely extinguish campfires before leaving.
  • Dispose of cigarettes properly.
  • Use fire-safe alternatives to fireworks and open flames.
  • Follow local burn bans and fire regulations.

Safer Alternatives

Instead of fireworks, try laser light shows or glow sticks. Replace open flame cooking with portable electric stoves or grills. Share fire safety tips with friends and family.
